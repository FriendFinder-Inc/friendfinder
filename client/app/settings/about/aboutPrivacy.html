FAQ

Why do I have to login through Facebook?

All friendfinder users must have a Facebook account to validate their identity and to provide their friend connections. We use these connections to allow users to search for people with mutual friends.

Are you going to post on my timeline?

No, friendfinder will never post to your timeline. Facebook may display that you’ve downloaded the app, but you can prevent this by changing the visibility settings to “Only Me” in the app center preferences.

Is my activity within friendfinder private?

By default your friendfinder profile is not viewable by your Facebook friends from within the website. Also all profiles are only viewable from within the website, and not public to the world.

What do you do with my information?

All information provided to friendfinder through Facebook (including your photos and email address) are never sold or distributed to any third parties. friendfinder uses this information exclusively to make your experience on friendfinder better. You can disconnect friendfinder from your Facebook account at any time, and friendfinder will immediately delete all of your personal information.

Why do you ask for access to my email address?

To send you emails, of course! By default an email is sent to you when someone messages you, but you can easily disable this feature from the settings page.

Why do you ask for access to my pictures?

So we can display your photos on your friendfinder profile. Currently friendfinder displays your 8 most recent profile photos, but we have a photo selector on the agenda. Stay tuned!

What about the rest of my info?

Your work history, education history, likes etc. are used to help users with similar interests to yourself find you in a search, and to let you find those users. The more information you share, the better matches you can find using friendfinder.

Can I permanently remove my information and deauthorize my account?

Of course. To uninstall friendfinder, go to friendfinder.io, and on the settings page, choose ‘deactivate account’. This will remove you completely from our database. Then go to https://facebook.com/appcenter/my from a desktop computer (will not work from mobile device) and remove the app from the list.
